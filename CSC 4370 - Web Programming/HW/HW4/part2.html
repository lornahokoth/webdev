• Select the number of pictures you want to play with
    • 8 Picutres - 120 seconds to find all matches
    • 10 Pictures - 150 seconds to find all matches
    • 12 Pictures - 180 seconds to find all matches
  • Select your difficulty
    • Easy - 8 seconds to memorize the board
    • Medium - 5 seconds to memorize the board
    • Hard - 3 seconds to memorize the board
  • Click a number to flip a card
  • When two cards are flipped
    • If the numbers match they will remain visible
    • If the numbers don't match they will be flipped back over
  • The game ends when you match all pictures or run out of time
